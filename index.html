    About Us

    YANTRIK Technologies is an engineering and automation solution-providing company. The Company's Corporate Office and Manufacturing plant is located in Faridabad, Haryana. We have an engineering branch and manufacturing plant in Pune, Maharashtra, INDIA.

    YANTRIK is led by an invincible combination of young minds and grey hair, our think-tank is equipped with more than two decades of maturity on one hand and vibrant energy on the other. This we believe is an apt combination to execute every project with a motto: “ Quality, Delivered on Time ”.

    YANTRIK Technologies emphasizes providing High-End Design and Engineering Solutions to various customers across automotive, non-automotive, and general manufacturing industries.

    Our Mission

    YANTRIK's mission is to use our extensive experience to deliver tangible business results enabling our clients in industry. With our core belief, we strive to build long-term client relationships based on Results, Mutual Trust and Respect.
